Scott Disick Parties in NYC With 18-Year-Old Model by His Side: Photos

Lord knows what happened here. Single Scott Disick partied it up in NYC this week with a mystery blonde by his side, who was eventually identified as an 18-year-old model named Lindsay Vrckovnik.

Kourtney Kardashian‘s ex, 32, was photographed on Wednesday, Sept. 30, galavanting around NYC’s East Village with a leggy platinum blonde.

Entertainment Tonight later ID’d the model as Vrckovnik, a current Parsons School of Design student who is 14 years younger than Disick. According to ET, Vrckovnik graduated high school in 2015, and is also a visual artist.

Earlier this year, she spoke to a blog, Daughter Runs, about developing her photography and paintings. “Most of my inspiration comes from my environment, and the people I’m surrounded by,” she told the site. “Nothing inspires me more than being in a creative environment, or seeing someone engaging in something they’re passionate about (thus why NYC is the dream ahhh).”

According to TMZ, the two were first spotted walking out from Trump SoHo on Wednesday, Sept. 30. The party continued throughout the day and ended at 1 OAK Nightclub, where Snoop Dogg played his set.

A source tells Us that Disick is pals with Snoop, and was behind the booth with the rapper, who goes by DJ Snoopadelic when he spins. The insider tells Us that Disick wasn’t drinking booze at the club, and that things were generally “low-key” at 1 OAK.

After his club stop, he and Vrckovnik hit an afterparty at Soho House, which didn’t wind down until 6 a.m.

As reported by Us Weekly last month, Disick hooked up with Kourtney’s longtime friend Kimberly Stewart — but ended the fling once word got out. “He was trying to get Kourtney back,” a source told Us at the time. “But he is hoping she doesn’t find out. He’s kicking himself.”
