Career

With a business and marketing degree and her father’s network of connections, Margaret quickly established a strong foothold in the business world. Soon after graduating from New York University, Margaret joined 3sixty Hospitality.

At age 26, Margaret became the marketing executive of 3sixty Hospitality. The company deals with providing quality catering services to its customers and has partnered with Lotus and Double Seven to provide a fine-dining experience to its high-profile clients.

While Lotus is a restaurant and nightclub having branches in Germany, the USA, and France, Double Seven is a New York-based cocktail lounge.

Margaret has always been an avid foodie. She appeared on the show The Kitchen from 2016 to 2017. Her experience as a marketing executive for 3sixty definitely must have helped Margaret make an easy transition to TV.

Apart from this, Margaret Anne has also been a part of the 86th Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade. The show went on to be nominated for Daytime Creative Arts Emmy Awards.

In addition, Margaret Anne has also been a producer for shows such as The Great American Foodathon, The Great New York Foodathon: A Star-Studded Special to Benefit City Harvest, and Chopped.

Since Margaret is the wife of Iron Chef Geoffrey Zakarian, it did not surprise many when the beautiful lady forayed into the restaurant business. The NYU graduate opened a restaurant called “Town” with her husband, Geoffrey. Their business proved to be extremely successful, as the restaurant is known to serve over a
hundred customers every day.

Soon after their marriage, Geoffrey and Margaret decided to open another restaurant named “Country.” This Manhattan restaurant, established in 2005, proved even more successful than the first one since “Country” was awarded a Michelin Star.
