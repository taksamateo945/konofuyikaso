Kim Kardashian Reveals New Hair Color Change: Platinum Blonde to White

Never satisfied! After shocking fans with her new platinum blonde locks on Thursday, March 5, Kim Kardashian took it a step further on Saturday, March 7, dyeing her hair a second time. The reality star, 34, was spotted exiting the Paris salon L’Atelier with hair and scalp practically white. See more pics of Kardashian’s new hair.
