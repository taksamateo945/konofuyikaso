Was Amy Roloff Unreasonable About The Family Farm?

The Little People, Big World spoilers tease whether Amy Roloff was being unreasonable about the family farm. This brought up a heavy debate on Reddit. One fan sided with her ex-husband Matt. Keep on reading to learn more.

Once again, this discussion was brought up on Reddit. One fan wanted to know if Amy Roloff was being unreasonable during Little People, Big World Season 18, Episode 10 when Amy, Matt, and Zach discussed the future of the family farm. Both Matt and Amy wanted the best for their family home and farm, but Amy still lived in the house.

Matt wanted to build a new home on the farm. He still wanted Amy’s input even though they were divorced. His sons went along with the idea, but Amy wasn’t on board. She argued that Matt would have to use his money to build a new house.

LPBW Spoilers: Was Amy Roloff Unreasonable?

The Little People, Big World spoilers reveal that she wasn’t going to invest in her ex-husband’s new home. The original poster asked whether she was being unfair or rational. Also, whether it was fair that she still lived in the big house? Other LPBW fans took to the thread to share their thoughts.

According to the Little People, Big World spoilers, Amy was being reasonable for obvious reasons. She had no right to split the costs of Matt’s new home, especially since they were divorced. Unfortunately, their divorce agreement would’ve made things easier for both of them.

Amy Roloff hadn’t agreed to split on a new home on the farm. The original poster argued that they could see both Amy’s and Matt’s sides of this argument. However, the overwhelming majority of fans sided in Amy’s favor. They don’t believe she should be responsible for her ex-husband’s house, especially when he was unfaithful.

Little People, Big World: Matt Was Unfaithful

The Little People, Big World spoilers tease that Matt was unfaithful to Amy throughout their marriage. Fans argued that he didn’t treat her well. He even tried to screw her over in the divorce, but it didn’t work. She didn’t cave into his ways.

LPBW fans noticed that Matt dragged Amy through the mud. It was obvious as to why Amy hated her ex-husband. Matt’s true colors came out in their divorce. It even caused a rift among the Roloff family, which affected the future of their farm.
