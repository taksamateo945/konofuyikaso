Kendall &amp; Kylie's New Topshop Collection is Here!

Kendall & Kylie Jenner have just revealed their second collection for Topshop – and it’s even better than the first.

The Kendall + Kylie holiday collection is available in stores and online from this Friday (20th November) and follows the sisters’ hugely successful debut collection which launched back in June this year. “We’re so excited to to be designing another line for Topshop,” the youngest members of the Kardashin/Jenner family said, adding that they “made sure to include a unique selection of pieces, from glamorous to edgy” in their new offering.

Whilst their first collection perfectly captured the care-free vibes of summer, their new pieces fully embrace the spirit of the upcoming holiday season. The 15 piece collection is all about faux fur coats, metallic skirts and little black party dresses. When asked about their inspiration for the new line the sisters said: “We really wanted to showcase our personal style but mix it up with a holiday twist. The holidays are always so festive and of course, you have to have the right wardrobe for every occasion. We wanted to design a special line of pieces that capture both an elegant and edgy vibe.”

Kendall & Kylie’s own closets may be packed full of Balmain and Céline, but thankfully their own designs are set to retail at much more affordable prices than their designer favorites – all pieces in the new collection are priced between £30 and £130.
