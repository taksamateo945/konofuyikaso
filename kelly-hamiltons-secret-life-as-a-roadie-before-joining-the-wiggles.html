Kelly Hamilton's secret life as a roadie before joining The Wiggles

Kelly Hamilton has joined The Wiggles as part of the group’s diverse shake-up. 

And Daily Mail Australia can reveal the 45-year-old isn’t new to the spotlight, having worked as a roadie with touring bands for the past decade.

Kelly has toured Australia with the likes of Ice-T and his wife Coco Austin during their 2017 tour, and singer Grace Jones in 2018.

The Wiggles last week announced the hiring of four new ‘diverse’ members. 

Three women and a former pop singer will be joining Anthony Field, Simon Pryce, Emma and Lachlan Gillespie.

Kelly will be sharing the Wiggles’ stage with fellow newcomer Tsehay Hawkins, a 15-year-old who was adopted from Ethiopia. 

They will be joined by Justice Crew member John Pearce, a 30-year-old personal trainer with a Filipino background. 

Rounding out the new arrivals is Evie Ferris, a 24-year-old Indigenous dancer from Cairns.
