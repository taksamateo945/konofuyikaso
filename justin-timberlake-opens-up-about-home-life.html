Justin Timberlake opens up about Son, Silas

Justin Timberlake has been pretty quiet about his home life over the last few months since he became a first time father back in April after his wife Jessica Biel, who is an actress gave birth to their Son Silas.

Speaking to Jimmy Fallon on the Tonight Show, Timberlake opened up about becoming a father, saying that:

“It is the most insanely amazing, wonderful, unbelievable thing to ever happen to you.”

Whist Timberlake was on the show and glowing with pride he shared with the audience and viewers at home that his son Silas had already said his first word, which was ‘Dada’.

Timerlake also gave everyone the rare opportunity to see into his home life by sharing a selection of home photos of his son.
