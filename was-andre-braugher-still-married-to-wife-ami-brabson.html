Was Andre Braugher Still Married To Wife Ami Brabson?

Many fans and readers are eager to discover if Andre Braugher remained married to his wife, Ami Brabson, before his passing.

Andre Braugher, renowned for his roles in hit sitcoms like Brooklyn Nine-Nine and Homicide: Life on the Street, succumbed to a brief illness on December 11, 2023, as reported by Deadline.

The accomplished actor, aged 61, not only had a flourishing career but also a successful family life with his wife, Ami Brabson. The couple shared three children throughout their years together.

On December 12, Audra McDonald, who co-starred with Braugher on The Good Fight, expressed her condolences to his family.

In a heartfelt Instagram post, she reminisced about their collaboration, emphasizing his significant presence.

Was Andre Braugher still married to his wife before his death?

Yes, Andre Braugher remained married to his wife Ami Brabson, until his recent demise.

Who is Ami Brabson?

Ami Brabson, a skilled actress, appeared in various shows, including Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Kidnapped, Six Degrees, The Jury, and the iconic ABC serial opera All My Children. Additionally, she is a singer, producer, and theatrical actor, as detailed on her official website. Ami holds an MFA from the esteemed NYU/Tisch Graduate School of Acting. She and Andre initially met and starred together on Homicide: Life on the Street, marrying in 1991.

Did Andre and Ami have kids?

Yes, the couple had three children—Michael, Isaiah, and John—throughout their marriage. In a revealing 2020 interview, Andre shared that despite his award-winning career, he prioritized spending quality time with his sons, even flying from Los Angeles to New York during the filming of Brooklyn Nine-Nine. Reflecting on his choices, he acknowledged the potential for a more extensive career but questioned the personal sacrifices it might entail.
