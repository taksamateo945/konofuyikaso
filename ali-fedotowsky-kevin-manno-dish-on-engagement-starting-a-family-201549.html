Ali Fedotowsky, Kevin Manno Dish on Engagement, Starting a Family

A Bachelorette no more! As Us Weekly reported, Ali Fedotowsky announced on Thursday, Sept. 3, that she and her radio host boyfriend, Kevin Manno, are engaged—and the Bachelorette alum tells Us Weekly that they're already gearing up to start a family.

"Yesterday was the most incredible day of our lives. We cried, we laughed, our dog Owen wore a tux—hah!" Fedotowsky, 30, who famously competed on the Bachelor before becoming the sixth Bachelorette, reveals to Us. "It was such a special moment, just the three of us in our new home."

A special moment that came with some very special bling! Manno, 32, who hosts Valentine in the Morning on L.A.'s 104.3 radio station, popped the question with a huge Neil Lane diamond engagement ring, featuring a whopping center sparkler surrounded by smaller stones.

But while the TV correspondent revealed via her personal blog, Ali Luvs,  that she "screamed yes!" she also tells Us that there's no date planned—yet. "[I have] no idea when we will get married," Fedotowsky says. But she adds, "We want to start growing our little family soon, so it will either be soon or we'll have a happy, long engagement."
