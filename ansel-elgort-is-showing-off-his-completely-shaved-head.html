Ansel Elgort is Showing Off His Completely Shaved Head

Ansel Elgort is sporting a new look these days!

Just recently, one of the 27-year-old Baby Driver‘s friends took to Instagram to share a selfie with Ansel, who made a big change to his hair.

Click inside to see the pic!

In the photo – which you can see down below – Ansel is sporting a completely shaved buzz cut hair style.

The photo was first re-shared by popular celeb gossip Instagram, @DeuxMoi, which also shared a report that Ansel is apparently working on a new show for HBO Max.
